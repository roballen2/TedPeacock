Lesson 2 - Open Stroke

The Open Stroke

The single stroke begins with the stick tip up and finishes with the tip back at the top. Use your wrist to move the stick by rotating the hand around the stick's fulcrum, or pivot point between the first knuckle of the index finger and the second knuckle of the middle finger.
