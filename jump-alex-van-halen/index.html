Jump - Alex Van Halen

Click the image above to download the transcription (PDF).

One of my students was inspired to start playing drums after he saw Sina performing a YouTube play-along to Van Halen's "Jump". Alex Van Halen always provides cool grooves and variations. His plays with a real sense of swing - a great feel for the eighth note. The pre-chorus and solo section in this tune are a perfect example of Alex Van Halen's inventive drumming.
