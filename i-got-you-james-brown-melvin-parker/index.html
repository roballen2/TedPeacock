Transcription

I Got You (I Feel Good) - Melvin Parker

Click the image above to download the transcription (PDF).

One of the most requested songs on the club circuit and a pocket that won't quit. I transcribed this tune just to get the details of Melvin Parker's cross-stick groove in the chorus and the hyped up verse double backbeat with fills. If you want to learn more about "I Got You (I Feel Good)" and other classic James Brown's rhythm section grooves and arrangements check out this book:

The Funk Masters: The Great James Brown Rhythm Sections 1960-1973 by Allan "Dr. Licks" Slutsky and Chuck Silverman. Alfred Publishing Co., Inc.
